Who can play?

No matter what your skill level is, you're welcome to join our team. You must be ages 12-18 to be on the youth team.

What do I wear?

Once you've joined the team, you'll need to wear the folllowing to all practices and games

  • Team jersey
  • Grey trousers
  • Black long socks
  • Baseball glove
  • Baseball cleats
  • Jockstrap & protective cup *
  • Helmet and Bat

* if required
